<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Type</li><li>Thermoplastic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical carbon fiber composites market is categorized primarily into thermosetting and thermoplastic types. Thermosetting composites are characterized by irreversible hardening when cured, offering superior rigidity and resistance to deformation, making them ideal for load-bearing applications in medical devices. In contrast, thermoplastic composites can be reprocessed and reshaped upon heating, providing greater flexibility and ease of manufacturing. Both types are crucial in enhancing the performance and durability of medical equipment, prosthetics, and other healthcare applications.</p></p>
