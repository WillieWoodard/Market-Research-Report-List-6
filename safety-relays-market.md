<p><p>Safety relays are electrical devices designed to control and monitor safety functions in industrial equipment. There are three main types of safety relays on the market: electromechanical relay, solid state relay, and thermal relay. Electromechanical relays use mechanical contacts to turn circuits on and off, solid state relays use semiconductor components for switching, and thermal relays rely on temperature changes to interrupt the circuit. Each type of relay has its own unique characteristics and applications in ensuring the safety of industrial machinery.</p></p>
