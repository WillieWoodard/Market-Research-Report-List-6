<p><p>The Small Business Network Switches market consists of two main types: fixed port switches and modular switches. Fixed port switches are designed with a set number of Ethernet ports, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for small to medium-sized businesses. In contrast, modular switches provide flexibility, allowing users to customize the number of ports and features by adding or changing modules as needed. This adaptability makes modular switches suitable for growing businesses with evolving network requirements, balancing initial investment and long-term scalability.</p></p>
