<p><p>Industrial encapsulation adhesives are used to protect electronic components from environmental factors such as moisture, dust, and chemicals. The market is divided into heat-cured adhesives, which require heat for curing, and light-cured adhesives, which cure when exposed to ultraviolet light. Heat-cured adhesives are suitable for high-volume production due to their faster curing time, while light-cured adhesives are preferred for their ability to provide precise and controlled curing. Both types offer excellent adhesion and protection for electronic devices.</p></p>
