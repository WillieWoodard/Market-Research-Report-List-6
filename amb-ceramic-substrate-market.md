<p><p>The AMB (Active Metal Bonding) Ceramic Substrate market is primarily segmented into two types: SiN (Silicon Nitride) AMB substrates and AlN (Aluminum Nitride) AMB substrates. SiN AMB substrates are known for their excellent thermal and electrical insulating properties, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, AlN AMB substrates offer superior thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-power devices. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, supporting advanced packaging solutions.</p></p>
