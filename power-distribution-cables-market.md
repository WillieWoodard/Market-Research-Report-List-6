<p><strong>The Power Distribution Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HV</li><li>MV</li><li>LV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power distribution cables market can be categorized into three main types based on voltage levels - High Voltage (HV), Medium Voltage (MV), and Low Voltage (LV). HV cables are used for transmitting electricity over long distances at high voltages, typically above 33 kV. MV cables are used for distribution within industrial and commercial buildings at voltages between 1 kV and 33 kV. LV cables are used for distributing electricity to residential homes and small commercial buildings at voltages below 1 kV.</p></p>

<p><p>Power distribution cables are used in three main applications: overland, underground, and submarine. Overland cables are typically used to transmit power above ground, often in rural or remote areas. Underground cables are buried underground for urban and densely populated areas to distribute power without the need for overhead lines. Submarine cables are installed underwater to connect islands or to transmit power between countries. Each application serves a specific purpose in ensuring the efficient distribution of electricity in various environments.</p></p>
