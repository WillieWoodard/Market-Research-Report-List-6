<p><p>CNC lapping polishing machines are categorized into single surface and double surface types, each serving distinct applications. Single surface lapping polishing machines focus on achieving precision on one side of a material, making them ideal for components requiring a specific flatness or finish on a single side. Conversely, double surface lapping polishing machines are designed to process two surfaces simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and consistency in thickness and finish. These machines are crucial in industries requiring high precision, such as optics and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
