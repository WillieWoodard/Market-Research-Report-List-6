<p><p>The modified epoxy resin market comprises two primary types: Glycidyl Ether Resins and Glycidyl Lipid Resins. Glycidyl Ether Resins are known for their excellent mechanical properties and adhesion, making them suitable for coatings, adhesives, and composites. Glycidyl Lipid Resins, derived from natural fats and oils, offer enhanced flexibility and lower viscosity, making them ideal for applications in bio-based products and environmentally friendly formulations. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
