<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Compact Industrial Metal AM Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF)</li><li>Material Extrusion (ME)</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition (DED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) is a type of metal additive manufacturing process in which a high-powered laser fuses metal powder together layer by layer. Material Extrusion (ME) involves feeding metal wire or filament through a heated nozzle to create the desired shape. Directed Energy Deposition (DED) uses a focused energy source, such as a laser or electron beam, to melt and fuse metal powder or wire onto a substrate. Each of these market types offers unique benefits and applications for producing metal components with a compact industrial metal AM printer.</p></p>
