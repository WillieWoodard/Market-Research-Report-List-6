<p><strong>The TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TVS market can be categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types based on their voltage operation. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against voltage transients in a single polarity direction, typically suited for unidirectional circuits. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can manage transients in both positive and negative voltage directions, making them ideal for bidirectional circuits. Both types are essential in safeguarding electronic components from voltage surges, ensuring reliability and longevity in various applications.</p></p>
