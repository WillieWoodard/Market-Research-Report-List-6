<p><p>Automotive Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) is a critical technology used to control nitrogen oxide (NOx) emissions from diesel engines. By injecting a urea-based solution, known as diesel exhaust fluid (DEF), into the exhaust stream, SCR converts harmful NOx gases into harmless nitrogen and water vapor, thereby helping vehicles meet stringent emission standards. The increasing regulatory pressures worldwide for lower emissions, combined with the growth of diesel vehicle sales, are driving the demand for SCR systems.</p><p>The Automotive SCR Market is experiencing robust growth, influenced by the rising adoption of diesel technology and the need for compliance with environmental regulations.
