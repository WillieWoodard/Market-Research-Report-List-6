FFDM uses digital detectors to capture breast images for enhanced clarity and efficiency, facilitating quicker diagnosis. In contrast, DBT provides a three-dimensional view of breast tissue, allowing radiologists to identify abnormalities more accurately while minimizing false positives. Both technologies play pivotal roles in early breast cancer detection, driving advancements in diagnostic imaging and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
