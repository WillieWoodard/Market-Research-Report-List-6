<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Glucometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Type Blood Glucometer</li><li>Electrodes Type Glucometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucometer market is primarily divided into two types: photoelectric type blood glucometers and electrodes type glucometers. Photoelectric glucometers utilize light absorption principles to measure glucose levels in the blood, providing non-invasive results. In contrast, electrodes type glucometers rely on electrochemical reactions between glucose and electrodes to determine blood sugar levels, typically requiring a small blood sample. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient diabetes management tools, each offering unique advantages within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
