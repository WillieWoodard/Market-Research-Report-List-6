<p><p>The Near Infrared Band Fiber Optical Spectrometer market is divided into two main types: Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers and Multimode Fiber Spectrometers. Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers utilize a single light path for minimal signal loss, offering high sensitivity and precision, ideal for applications requiring accurate spectral analysis. Conversely, Multimode Fiber Spectrometers support multiple light paths, enabling broader wavelength coverage and higher light collection efficiency, making them suitable for applications needing faster data acquisition and increased throughput.</p></p>
