<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Driving Recorder market is categorized into two main types: integrated and portable devices. Integrated driving recorders are built directly into the vehicle's design, offering seamless operation and connectivity with the car's systems. They typically provide enhanced functionalities, such as real-time monitoring and data analytics. Portable driving recorders, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be easily moved between vehicles. They offer flexibility and can be used independently, catering to users who may drive multiple cars or prefer a low-cost solution.</p></p>
