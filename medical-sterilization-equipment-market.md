<p><p>The medical sterilization equipment market comprises several key types: steam sterilizers, ethylene oxide sterilizers, and others. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to eliminate microbial life, making them effective for heat-resistant instruments. Ethylene oxide sterilizers employ ethylene oxide gas to sterilize heat-sensitive devices, ensuring effective microbial control without damaging materials. Other sterilization methods may include hydrogen peroxide vapor, ozone, and radiation, catering to specific requirements across healthcare settings like hospitals, clinics, and laboratories for optimal infection prevention.</p></p>
