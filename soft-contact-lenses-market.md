<p><strong>The Soft Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicone Hydrogel</li><li>Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft contact lens market primarily includes two main types: silicone hydrogel and traditional hydrogels. Silicone hydrogel lenses are known for their superior oxygen permeability, allowing more oxygen to reach the cornea, which enhances comfort and eye health. They are suitable for extended wear. In contrast, traditional hydrogels offer a softer feel and are often more affordable, but they have lower oxygen transmission. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing comfort, wear duration, and budget considerations.</p></p>
