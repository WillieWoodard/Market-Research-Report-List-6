<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED lighting driver ICs market is primarily segmented into two types: SMD (Surface-Mount Device) and through-hole types. SMD driver ICs are compact, enabling high-density circuit designs suitable for modern applications like smart lighting and signage. Through-hole driver ICs, on the other hand, provide robust connections and are easier to repair, making them suitable for industrial and high-power applications. Both types serve essential roles in optimizing LED performance, efficiency, and thermal management in various lighting solutions.</p></p>
