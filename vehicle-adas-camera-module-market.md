<p><p>The Vehicle Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) Camera Module market primarily consists of two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for applications requiring precise imaging in low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for a wide range of ADAS functions, including lane departure warnings and obstacle detection.</p></p>
