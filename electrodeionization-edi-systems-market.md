<p><p>Electrodeionization (EDI) Systems utilize two different types of membranes in the market: Homogeneous Membrane and Heterogeneous Membrane. Homogeneous Membrane systems consist of identical membranes throughout, ensuring consistent ion removal. On the other hand, Heterogeneous Membrane systems incorporate a combination of different membranes to enhance selectivity and efficiency in ion removal. Both types of EDI systems play a crucial role in water purification processes, providing reliable and effective solutions for various industries.</p></p>
