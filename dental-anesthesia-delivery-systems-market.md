<p><p>Computer-Controlled Local Anesthetic Delivery Systems (CCLAD) are a type of dental anesthesia delivery system that allows for precise control and dosage of anesthesia, resulting in more comfortable and efficient delivery for patients. Jet Injectors use high-pressure streams of anesthesia to quickly and effectively numb the injection site. Intra-Osseous Anesthesia Systems (IO) deliver anesthesia directly into the bone, offering a more targeted and efficient method of pain management during dental procedures. Each of these systems offers unique benefits for both patients and practitioners.</p></p>
