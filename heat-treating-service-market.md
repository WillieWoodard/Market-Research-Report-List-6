<p><p>The heat treating service market comprises two primary segments: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment involves processes such as annealing, quenching, and tempering to enhance the overall properties of materials, typically steel and alloys. In contrast, surface heat treatment focuses on altering the surface characteristics of components, including hardening or softening, to improve wear resistance, fatigue strength, and corrosion resistance. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
