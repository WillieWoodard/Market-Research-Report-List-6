<p><p>Full Face Coverage Type firefighting helmets offer complete protection to the firefighter's head and face during operations. They are designed to shield against heat, flames, and debris. On the other hand, Half Face Helmet Type helmets provide protection primarily to the top and sides of the head, leaving the face partially exposed. They are lighter and offer better ventilation but provide less comprehensive protection compared to Full Face Coverage Type helmets. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of firefighters based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
