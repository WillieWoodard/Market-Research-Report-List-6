<p><p>Armoured cables are categorized into two types - aluminium wire armour and steel wire armour. Aluminium wire armoured cables are lighter in weight and more flexible, making them easier to install. Both types of armoured cables are commonly used in industries for providing additional protection to electrical wires and ensuring a reliable power supply.</p></p>
