<p><strong>The Cable Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable sensors market consists of various types, primarily including NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors, along with other variants. NTC sensors decrease resistance as temperature rises, making them ideal for precise temperature monitoring in various applications. In contrast, PTC sensors increase resistance with temperature, offering benefits in overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating. Other types may include thermocouples and fiber optic sensors, each serving specialized roles in industries like automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
