<p><p>The automotive torque converter parts market can be categorized into three main types: housing, turbine, and others. The housing component of a torque converter is responsible for enclosing all the internal components and providing structural support. The turbine is the part of the torque converter that is driven by the transmission fluid and transfers power to the transmission input shaft. The "others" category includes various smaller components such as the stator, impeller, and clutch plates that contribute to the overall efficiency and performance of the torque converter.</p></p>
