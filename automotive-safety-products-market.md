<p><p>Automotive safety products can be categorized into active and passive safety systems. Active safety products are designed to prevent accidents from happening in the first place, such as antilock braking systems, electronic stability control, and adaptive headlights. On the other hand, passive safety products are meant to protect occupants in the event of a collision, like airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both types of safety products play a crucial role in ensuring the well-being of drivers and passengers on the road.</p></p>
