<p><strong>The Aircraft Weighing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft weighing equipment market includes two main types: platform and floor-standing scales. Platform scales are designed for weighing larger aircraft, offering a stable surface that can support the entire weight, often used in maintenance and inspection. Floor-standing scales, on the other hand, are more versatile and suited for smaller aircraft or components. They are typically easier to relocate and can be used for various weighing tasks, making them popular in smaller hangars and maintenance facilities. Both types ensure precision and compliance with aviation standards.</p></p>
