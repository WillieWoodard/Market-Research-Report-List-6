<p><p>Building isolation bearings are important structural components that help absorb shock, dampen vibration, and allow movement in buildings. There are two main types of building isolation bearings: Elastomeric bearings, which are made of rubber or similar materials and provide flexibility and energy dissipation, and sliding bearings, which use sliding surfaces to accommodate movement. Both types are crucial for protecting buildings from seismic forces and ensuring structural stability during natural disasters or high-impact events.</p></p>
