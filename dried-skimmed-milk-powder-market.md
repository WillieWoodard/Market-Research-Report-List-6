<p><p>The dried skimmed milk powder (SMP) market is categorized into three types based on the heat treatment process: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP undergoes minimal heat processing, preserving its nutritional quality and flavor, ideal for dairy products. Medium Heat SMP has a moderate heat treatment, balancing heat stability and nutritional value, suitable for various applications. High Heat SMP experiences extensive heating, enhancing shelf life, making it suitable for baked goods and long-term storage.</p></p>
