<p><ul><li>Constant Current</li><li>Regulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC LED driver market comprises two primary types: constant current and regulated drivers. Constant current drivers maintain a steady output current, ensuring LED longevity and consistent brightness, making them ideal for applications where precise light control is crucial. Regulated drivers, on the other hand, adjust their output voltage to stabilize power supply fluctuations, accommodating varying LED configurations. Both types are essential in enhancing LED performance across diverse applications, from general lighting to automotive and display technologies.</p></p>
