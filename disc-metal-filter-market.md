<p><ul><li>Wire Mesh</li><li>Laser Drilled Holes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disc metal filters are commonly categorized into two types - wire mesh filters and laser drilled hole filters. </p><p>Wire mesh filters are made of woven metal wires and are known for their durability and strength. They are primarily used for large particle filtration.</p><p>Laser drilled hole filters, on the other hand, are created by using laser technology to create precise holes in metal discs. These filters are ideal for fine particle filtration and offer high levels of precision and efficiency.</p></p>
