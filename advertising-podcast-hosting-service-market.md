<p><ul><li>Host–Read Ad</li><li>Programmatic Ad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advertising podcast hosting services primarily encompass two market types: host-read ads and programmatic ads. Host-read ads involve the podcast host directly delivering promotional messages, creating a personal connection with the audience, enhancing trust, and increasing engagement. In contrast, programmatic ads use automated technology to insert targeted advertisements into podcast episodes, often based on listener data. This allows for dynamic ad placements, ensuring relevant content is delivered, but may lack the personal touch associated with host-read promotions.</p></p>
