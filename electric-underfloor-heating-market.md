<p><strong>The Electric Underfloor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heating Cable</li><li>Electrothermal</li><li>Carbon Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric underfloor heating market can be categorized into three main types: heating cable, electrothermal, and carbon crystal. Heating cable systems consist of cables installed beneath the floor to generate heat. Electrothermal systems use conductive polymers to heat the floor surface. Carbon crystal systems utilize a thin layer of conductive carbon crystals to provide radiant heat. Each type of system offers its own unique advantages in terms of installation ease, energy efficiency, and overall effectiveness in heating indoor spaces.</p></p>
