<p><strong>The Interference Optical Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Pass Type</li><li>Low-Pass Type</li><li>Band-Pass Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interference optical filters are utilized in various applications based on their transmission characteristics. High-pass filters allow wavelengths above a certain threshold to pass while blocking lower wavelengths, ideal for applications needing specific light isolation. Low-pass filters do the opposite, transmitting low wavelengths and obstructing higher ones, suitable for filtering out unwanted high-frequency signals. Band-pass filters transmit a specific range of wavelengths, blocking both lower and higher frequencies, commonly used in spectroscopy and telecommunications for precise wavelength selection.</p></p>
