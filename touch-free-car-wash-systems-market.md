<p><p>The touch-free car wash systems market includes Gantry Car Wash Systems and Conveyor Tunnel Car Wash Systems. Gantry systems utilize an overhead framework to move high-pressure water jets and cleaning solutions across the vehicle, minimizing physical contact and reducing the risk of damage. In contrast, Conveyor Tunnel systems employ a moving belt that carries vehicles through a series of cleaning stages, utilizing a combination of brushes and chemicals, though often designed to limit direct contact. Both systems aim to provide efficient and effective vehicle cleaning solutions.</p></p>
