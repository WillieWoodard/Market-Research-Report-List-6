<p><p>Cloud-based backup services can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Public Cloud**: Data is stored on third-party servers accessible over the internet, offering cost-effectiveness and scalability for users without extensive IT infrastructure.</p><p>2. **Private Cloud**: Backup services are hosted on dedicated infrastructure, providing enhanced security and control, ideal for businesses with strict compliance requirements.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud**: Combines public and private clouds, allowing organizations to balance cost and control, leveraging public resources for non-sensitive data while keeping critical information secured in a private environment.</p></p>
