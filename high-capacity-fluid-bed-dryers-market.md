<p><p>High-capacity fluid-bed dryers are essential in various industries for efficient drying of bulk materials. Static fluid-bed dryers operate by passing hot air through a stationary bed of material, ensuring uniform drying but with limited mixing. In contrast, vibrating fluid-bed dryers utilize vibrations to enhance material movement, promoting better heat transfer and quicker drying times. Both types cater to different operational needs, with static models favoring simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while vibrating systems offer improved efficiency and versatility for challenging materials.</p></p>
