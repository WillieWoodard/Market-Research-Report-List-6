Short Baseline (SBL) systems offer medium-range tracking capabilities with higher accuracy than USBL. Long Baseline (LBL) systems are ideal for long-range positioning of underwater assets with ultra-high accuracy. Each type caters to specific requirements and applications in industries such as oil & gas, defense, and marine research.</p></p>
