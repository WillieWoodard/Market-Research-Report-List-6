Lead Content below 55% Pb is considered lower lead glass, commonly used for everyday household items such as windows and containers. Lead Content between 55% to 65% Pb is known as medium lead glass, often used for decorative purposes like crystal glassware. Lead Content above 65% Pb falls under high lead glass, prized for its high clarity and brilliance, commonly used in fine art and restoration projects.</p></p>
