<p><p>Active Geofencing Market includes two types- Fixed and Mobile. Fixed geofencing involves setting up a virtual boundary around a specific location, such as a store or event venue, to target customers within that area. Mobile geofencing, on the other hand, uses the location of a user's mobile device to deliver targeted advertisements or notifications based on their real-time location. Both types aim to engage customers with relevant content and drive foot traffic to specific locations.</p></p>
