<p><p>Industrial metal 3D printer market types include Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM technology uses a high-powered laser to melt and fuse metal powder together layer by layer, while EBM technology uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder. Other types of metal 3D printing technologies include Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Binder Jetting. Each technology offers unique benefits and applications in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
