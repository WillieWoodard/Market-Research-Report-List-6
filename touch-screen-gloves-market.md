<p><ul><li>Single Layered Touchscreen Gloves</li><li>Double Layered Touchscreen Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Layered Touchscreen Gloves are gloves designed with a single layer of conductive material on the fingertips, allowing users to operate touchscreens without removing their gloves. These gloves are thinner and more lightweight, providing better dexterity but less insulation. On the other hand, Double Layered Touchscreen Gloves have an additional insulating layer, providing better warmth and protection in cold weather. However, they may reduce touch sensitivity and dexterity. Both types cater to different consumer preferences based on their priorities for warmth, dexterity, and touchscreen functionality.</p></p>
