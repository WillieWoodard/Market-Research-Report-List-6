<p><ul><li>Polymeric Type</li><li>Monomeric Type</li><li>Oligomeric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are categorized into three types based on their molecular structure. Polymeric HALS are large molecules that provide long-lasting protection and are often used in coatings and plastics to enhance durability. Monomeric HALS are smaller and more volatile, suitable for applications requiring quick stabilization but with shorter effectiveness. Oligomeric HALS strike a balance, offering enhanced stabilization compared to monomeric types while maintaining some of the longevity and effectiveness of polymeric options. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
