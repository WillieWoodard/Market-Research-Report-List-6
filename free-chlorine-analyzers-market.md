<p><ul><li>X-ray Fluorescence</li><li>UV Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The free chlorine analyzers market features two distinct technologies: X-ray fluorescence (XRF) and UV fluorescence. XRF utilizes X-ray technology to quantify chlorine levels in various samples, offering high precision and minimal interference. In contrast, UV fluorescence relies on the property of chlorine to absorb UV light and re-emit it, allowing for rapid and efficient measurement of free chlorine concentrations. Together, these methods address diverse applications in water treatment, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes, enhancing safety and compliance standards.</p></p>
