<p><p>Ultrasound imaging market encompasses various types such as 2D, 3D, 4D, Doppler imaging, and others. 2D imaging provides a two-dimensional image of the internal structures of the body. 3D imaging offers a three-dimensional view, providing more detailed and comprehensive images. 4D imaging adds the element of time, allowing for real-time visualization of moving structures. Doppler imaging is used to assess blood flow within the body. Other types in the market may include contrast-enhanced imaging or elastography for specialized diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
