<p><p>PET-CT systems are categorized into two main market types: fixed and mobile systems. Fixed PET-CT systems are permanently installed in medical facilities, offering high-resolution imaging and advanced diagnostic capabilities, often used in hospitals for comprehensive patient assessments. In contrast, mobile PET-CT systems are transportable units that can be deployed across multiple locations, providing flexible imaging solutions to underserved areas. This mobility enhances accessibility to diagnostic services, particularly in rural or remote regions where permanent installations may not be feasible.</p></p>
