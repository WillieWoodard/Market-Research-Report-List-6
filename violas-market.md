<p><p>Violas come in two main market types: Acoustic Violas and Electric Violas. Acoustic Violas produce sound through the resonance of the hollow body, making them a popular choice for classical musicians and traditional ensembles. On the other hand, Electric Violas have pickups that convert string vibrations into electrical signals, allowing for amplification and experimentation with different sounds. Electric Violas are often favored by contemporary musicians in genres like rock, jazz, and electronic music due to their versatility and ability to be modified with effects pedals.</p></p>
