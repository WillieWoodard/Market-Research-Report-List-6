<p><p>The electric ceramic ball valve market is categorized into three types based on temperature resistance: low temperature valves, normal temperature valves, and high temperature valves. Low temperature valves are designed for environments below zero degrees Celsius, ensuring efficient flow control in cold conditions. Normal temperature valves operate effectively within a moderate temperature range, suitable for typical applications. High temperature valves can withstand extreme heat, making them ideal for industrial processes that involve high-temperature fluids or gases, ensuring durability and performance.</p></p>
