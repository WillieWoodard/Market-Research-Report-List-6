<p><p>Hydrant Pit Valve Market includes two main types: Pneumatic and Manual. On the other hand, Manual hydrant pit valves require physical manipulation to adjust the water flow, offering a more traditional and reliable method of operation. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements, with pneumatic valves being more suitable for rapid response situations and manual valves providing a simpler and more cost-effective option.</p></p>
