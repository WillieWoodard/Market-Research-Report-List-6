<p><p>Flotation cells are used in mineral processing to separate valuable minerals from ore. Cell-to-cell flotation cells involve mixing and aeration within individual cells, leading to efficient separation. Free-flow flotation cells allow for continuous flow of pulp through the entire cell volume, increasing throughput and reducing maintenance. Both types of cells are crucial in the mining industry for achieving high recovery rates and maximizing profits. The choice between cell-to-cell and free-flow flotation cells depends on the specific needs of the operation.</p></p>
