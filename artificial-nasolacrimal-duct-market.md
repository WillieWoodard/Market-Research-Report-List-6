<p><p>The artificial nasolacrimal duct market encompasses devices designed to treat nasolacrimal duct obstructions. Two main types are monos canalicular stents and canalicular stents. Mono canalicular stents are designed to be inserted into a single canaliculus, allowing for straightforward placement and dilation of the duct. In contrast, canalicular stents can traverse both canaliculi, providing a more extensive solution for managing tear drainage issues. Both types aim to restore tear flow, improving patient comfort and reducing complications associated with tear duct obstructions.</p></p>
