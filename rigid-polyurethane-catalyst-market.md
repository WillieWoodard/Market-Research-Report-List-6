<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid polyurethane catalyst market is divided into two primary types: non-reactive amine catalysts and reactive amine catalysts. Non-reactive amine catalysts facilitate the polyurethane formulation process without participating in the polymerization reaction, enhancing the curing speed. In contrast, reactive amine catalysts actively engage in chemical reactions, influencing the final properties of the polyurethane product. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing performance attributes, affecting applications in construction, automotive, and various industrial sectors, ensuring efficient production processes.</p></p>
