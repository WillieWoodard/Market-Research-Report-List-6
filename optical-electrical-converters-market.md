<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode</li><li>Single Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical-electrical converters market is divided into multi-mode and single-mode categories. Multi-mode converters are designed for short-distance communication and can carry multiple light modes simultaneously, making them cost-effective for data centers and local area networks. In contrast, single-mode converters transmit light through a single waveguide, allowing for longer distances and higher bandwidth, which is ideal for telecommunications and long-haul applications. Each type serves specific needs based on distance, data rate, and application requirements.</p></p>
