<p><p>Cardiac ablation devices refer to devices used to treat abnormal heart rhythms by disrupting the abnormal electrical pathways in the heart tissue. Multielectrode ablation devices utilize multiple electrodes to create lesions, while single point ablation devices use a single electrode for the procedure. Multielectrode devices offer more precise and efficient treatment of complex arrhythmias, while single point devices are simpler and more cost-effective. Both types of devices play a crucial role in the growing market for cardiac ablation procedures.</p></p>
