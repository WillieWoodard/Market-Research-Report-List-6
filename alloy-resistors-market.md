<p><p>Alloy resistors are classified into different market types based on their composition and properties. The 0xxx market includes resistors made of metals like nickel-chromium, providing high stability and precision. The 1xxx market consists of resistors made of manganese-copper, offering low temperature coefficients. The 2xxx market includes resistors made of nickel-iron, providing high resistance to corrosion. The 4xxx market includes resistors made of nickel-cobalt, offering high temperature coefficients. The Others market encompasses all other alloy resistors not falling into the above categories.</p></p>
