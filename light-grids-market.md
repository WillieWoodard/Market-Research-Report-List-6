<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Grids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffraction Light Grids</li><li>Reflex Light Grids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light grids are advanced optical systems used for various applications including safety and measurement. Diffraction light grids utilize interference patterns created by light passing through slits, enabling precise positioning and detection. These are often employed in industrial automation and robotics for object detection. Reflex light grids, on the other hand, operate using mirrors to reflect beams back to their source, allowing for compact designs and simpler installation. Both types enhance operational efficiency in manufacturing, security, and motion detection systems.</p></p>
