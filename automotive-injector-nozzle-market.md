<p><p>The Automotive Injector Nozzle Market includes several types such as Sequential Fuel Injection (SFI), Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), SFI+GDI, and Diesel Direct Injection (DDI). SFI injects fuel into the intake ports using timed injectors, while GDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for improved performance and fuel efficiency. SFI+GDI combines the benefits of both systems for optimum performance. DDI is specifically designed for diesel engines, providing precise fuel delivery for better efficiency and reduced emissions.</p></p>
