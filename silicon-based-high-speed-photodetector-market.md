<p><p>The Silicon Based High Speed Photodetector market includes two main types: Silicon Drift Detector (SDD) and Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM). SDDs offer high sensitivity and high speed for X-ray and gamma-ray detection applications. SiPMs provide high photon detection efficiency and excellent timing resolution for low light-level applications, such as medical imaging and LiDAR systems. Both types of detectors are highly versatile and find applications in various fields including scientific research, healthcare, and industrial imaging.</p></p>
