<p><p>The titanium reactor market consists of three main types: pure titanium reactors, composite titanium reactors, and titanium-lined reactors. Pure titanium reactors are made entirely from titanium, offering high corrosion resistance and durability. Composite titanium reactors combine titanium with other materials to enhance specific properties while reducing costs. Titanium-lined reactors feature an interior lining of titanium, providing corrosion protection for cheaper substrates. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring optimal performance in challenging environments.</p></p>
