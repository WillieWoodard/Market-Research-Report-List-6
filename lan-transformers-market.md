<p><ul><li>PoE</li><li>Non-PoE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LAN Transformers market comprises two primary types: PoE (Power over Ethernet) and Non-PoE transformers. PoE transformers integrate power and data transmission over a single cable, making them ideal for powering devices like IP cameras and VoIP phones. In contrast, Non-PoE transformers provide data communication without power, typically used in traditional networking setups. Both types cater to different needs within network infrastructures, with PoE offering convenience and reduced cabling, while Non-PoE suits applications requiring separate power sources.</p></p>
