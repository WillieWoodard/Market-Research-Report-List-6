<p><ul><li>Coarse Grain WC</li><li>Fine Grain WC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbide blank market is primarily categorized into two types based on grain size: coarse grain tungsten carbide (WC) and fine grain tungsten carbide (WC). Coarse grain WC features larger grain structures, offering enhanced toughness and impact resistance, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications such as mining and machining. In contrast, fine grain WC possesses smaller grain structures, resulting in superior wear resistance and hardness, which is beneficial for precision cutting tools and applications requiring high performance and durability.</p></p>
