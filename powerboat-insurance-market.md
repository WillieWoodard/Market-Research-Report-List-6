<p><strong>The Powerboat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powerboat insurance typically comes in two market types: Actual Cash Value and Agreed Amount Value. Actual Cash Value policies cover the current market value of the boat at the time of a claim, taking into account depreciation. On the other hand, Agreed Amount Value policies allow the policyholder to agree on a specific value for the boat when taking out the policy, ensuring they will receive that agreed amount in the event of a total loss.</p></p>
