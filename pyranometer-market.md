<p><strong>The Pyranometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermopile Pyranometers</li><li>Photovoltaic Pyranometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pyranometer market primarily consists of two types: thermopile pyranometers and photovoltaic pyranometers. Thermopile pyranometers utilize thermoelectric sensors to measure solar radiation through temperature differentials, offering high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for various weather conditions. In contrast, photovoltaic pyranometers use silicon photovoltaic cells to directly convert solar energy into electrical signals, providing a more compact and cost-effective solution for solar monitoring. Both types are essential for solar energy applications, meteorology, and environmental studies.</p></p>
