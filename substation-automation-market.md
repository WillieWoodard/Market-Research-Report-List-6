<p><p>Substation automation market includes two main types: Transmission substations and Distribution substations. Transmission substations are responsible for transmitting high voltage electricity over long distances, typically from power plants to distribution substations. Distribution substations, on the other hand, are responsible for stepping down the voltage and distributing electricity to end users such as homes and businesses. Both types of substations require automation technologies to monitor and control the flow of electricity efficiently and safely.</p></p>
