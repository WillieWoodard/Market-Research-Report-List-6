<p><p>Disposable transfer pipettes are available in two main types: disposable graduated transfer pipettes and disposable nongraduated transfer pipettes. Graduated transfer pipettes have measurement markings along the length of the pipette, allowing for precise volume control during transfer. On the other hand, nongraduated transfer pipettes do not have measurement markings and are often used for simple transfer tasks without the need for precise volume measurements. Both types of disposable transfer pipettes are commonly used in various industries including healthcare, research, and manufacturing.</p></p>
