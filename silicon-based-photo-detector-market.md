<p><p>The silicon-based photodetector market comprises several types, primarily including Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) and Silicon PhotoMultipliers (SiPMs). SDDs are known for their high energy resolution and low noise, making them ideal for X-ray and gamma-ray spectroscopy applications. In contrast, SiPMs offer high sensitivity and are capable of detecting single photons, making them suitable for applications in medical imaging, LIDAR, and particle physics. Both technologies significantly enhance performance in various sensor applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
