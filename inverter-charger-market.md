<p><p>Inverter charger market types include pure sine, modified sine, and split phase inverters. Pure sine inverters provide power that is similar to household electricity, making them ideal for sensitive electronics. Modified sine inverters are more affordable but may produce lower quality power output. Split phase inverters combine two separate power sources to provide both 120V and 240V power. Each type of inverter caters to different needs and preferences, with pure sine inverters being the most reliable and versatile option for various applications.</p></p>
