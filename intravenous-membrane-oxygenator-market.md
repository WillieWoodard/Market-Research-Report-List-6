<p><p>The Intravenous Membrane Oxygenator market primarily includes two types: Silicon Membrane Oxygenators and Hole Polypropylene Membrane Oxygenators. Silicon Membrane Oxygenators utilize silicon-based membranes for gas exchange, offering high gas transfer efficiency and biocompatibility, making them suitable for critical care settings. Conversely, Hole Polypropylene Membrane Oxygenators feature microporous polypropylene membranes that facilitate oxygenation while minimizing blood trauma. Both types cater to various clinical applications, ensuring improved oxygen delivery and patient outcomes during medical procedures requiring advanced respiratory support.</p></p>
