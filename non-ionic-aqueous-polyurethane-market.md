<p><strong>The Non-ionic Aqueous Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane</li><li>Bicomponent Aqueous Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-ionic Aqueous Polyurethane market is primarily divided into two types: single-component and bicomponent systems. Single-component aqueous polyurethane provides ease of application, as it requires no mixing before use, making it suitable for various coatings and adhesives. In contrast, bicomponent aqueous polyurethane systems consist of a resin and a hardener, which must be mixed prior to application, offering enhanced performance characteristics like increased durability and chemical resistance. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications in coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
