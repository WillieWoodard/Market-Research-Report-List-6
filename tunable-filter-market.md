<p><ul><li>Liquid Crystal Tunable Filters (LCTFs)</li><li>Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs)</li><li>Linear-Variable Tunable Filters (LVTFs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tunable filter market includes various types, each serving specific applications. Liquid Crystal Tunable Filters (LCTFs) use liquid crystal technology for precise wavelength selection in imaging and spectroscopy. Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) manipulate light via sound waves, enabling fast and versatile wavelength tuning suitable for laser applications. Linear-Variable Tunable Filters (LVTFs) provide a gradient of wavelengths across a surface, ideal for spectral scanning. Other emerging technologies include mechanical and digital filters, each contributing to advancements in optical filtering solutions.</p></p>
