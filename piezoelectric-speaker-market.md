<p><p>The piezoelectric speaker market is divided into two main types: active and passive piezo. Active piezo speakers incorporate built-in amplifiers, enabling them to generate sound directly from an electrical signal, offering ease of use and integration in various devices. In contrast, passive piezo speakers require an external amplifier to produce sound, making them more versatile but dependent on additional components for operation. Both types find applications in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial devices, catering to diverse sound requirements.</p></p>
