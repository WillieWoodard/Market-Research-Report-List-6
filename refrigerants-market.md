<p><p>The refrigerants market is primarily categorized into three types: fluorocarbons, inorganics, and hydrocarbons. Fluorocarbons, including HFCs and CFCs, are synthetic compounds widely used for their efficiency but face regulatory challenges due to environmental concerns. Inorganic refrigerants, such as ammonia and water, are known for their minimal environmental impact but require careful handling. Hydrocarbons, including propane and isobutane, are natural refrigerants that offer low global warming potential and are gaining popularity due to their sustainability. Each type has distinct applications and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
