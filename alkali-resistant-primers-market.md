<p><ul><li>Pure Acrylic</li><li>Solvent Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alkali resistant primers are used to protect surfaces from high pH levels. Two types of alkali resistant primers are Pure Acrylic and Solvent Mixture. Pure Acrylic primers are made of acrylic resins and provide excellent alkali resistance. They are preferred for interior applications. Solvent Mixture primers are made of a mixture of solvents and resins, providing good alkali resistance as well as improved flexibility and adhesion. They are commonly used for exterior applications where exposure to harsh conditions is a concern.</p></p>
