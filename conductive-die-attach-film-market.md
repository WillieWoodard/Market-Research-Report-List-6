<p><p>The Conductive Die Attach Film market is segmented into two main types: electro-conductive films and non-electro-conductive films. Electro-conductive films are used to ensure efficient thermal and electrical conductivity between semiconductor dies and substrates, making them essential in high-performance applications. In contrast, non-electro-conductive films are designed for applications where electrical isolation is crucial, providing thermal management without conducting electricity. Both types play vital roles in electronics packaging, catering to specific requirements based on thermal and electrical properties.</p></p>
