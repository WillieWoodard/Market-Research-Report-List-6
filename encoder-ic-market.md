<p><strong>The Encoder IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Encoder IC</li><li>Rotary Encoder IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encoder IC market can be categorized into two main types: linear encoder ICs and rotary encoder ICs. Linear encoder ICs are designed for measuring linear position, translating the movement into an electrical signal for applications in automation and robotics. In contrast, rotary encoder ICs measure angular position and are widely used in motor control, robotics, and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in precision motion control, providing feedback systems that enhance accuracy and reliability in various electronic devices.</p></p>
