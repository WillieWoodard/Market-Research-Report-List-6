<p><ul><li>Powder Products</li><li>Flakes Products</li><li>Boards Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vermiculite market comprises three main types: powder products, flake products, and board products. Powdered vermiculite is fine and lightweight, often used for insulation and as a growing medium. Flake products are larger pieces favored for horticulture and construction, providing excellent thermal insulation and lightweight characteristics. Board products, made from compressed vermiculite, serve as durable insulation panels suitable for high-temperature applications. Each type caters to different industries, including construction, agriculture, and manufacturing, highlighting vermiculite's versatility and utility.</p></p>
