<p><p>The high-performance Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) market is primarily segmented into two main components: gyroscopes and accelerometers. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity and help determine orientation and rotation, crucial for applications in aviation, robotics, and consumer electronics. Accelerometers, on the other hand, measure linear acceleration, aiding in position tracking, motion detection, and stability control in various devices. Together, these components enhance navigational systems, improve automation capabilities, and drive advancements in technology across multiple industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
