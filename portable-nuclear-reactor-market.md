<p><ul><li>Thermal-neutron Reactors</li><li>Fast-neutron Reactors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable nuclear reactors can be categorized into two main types: thermal-neutron reactors and fast-neutron reactors. Thermal-neutron reactors are designed to slow down neutrons to increase the probability of fission, making them more efficient for generating electricity. Fast-neutron reactors, on the other hand, use fast neutrons to sustain a fission chain reaction, offering advantages such as better fuel utilization and the ability to use alternative fuel sources. Both types of reactors have unique benefits and applications in the portable nuclear reactor market.</p></p>
