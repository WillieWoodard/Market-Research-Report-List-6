<p><p>The advanced cell culture market consists of two primary types: 2D and 3D cell cultures. </p><p>2D cell culture involves growing cells in a monolayer on flat surfaces, providing a basic understanding of cellular behavior but often lacking in mimicking the natural tissue environment. </p><p>In contrast, 3D cell culture encourages cells to grow in all directions, creating more realistic tissue architectures. This approach can enhance cell function, improve drug testing outcomes, and better replicate in vivo conditions, making it increasingly favored for research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
