<p><strong>The Mobile Mapping Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Mobile Mapping System</li><li>Backpack Mobile Mapping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Mobile Mapping Systems consist of devices mounted on vehicles, such as cars, trucks, or drones, equipped with sensors and cameras to collect data while in motion. This system is commonly used for large-scale mapping projects. On the other hand, Backpack Mobile Mapping Systems are wearable devices that are carried by individuals walking on foot to capture data in areas inaccessible by vehicles. This system is ideal for mapping indoor spaces, narrow alleys, and pedestrian-only areas.</p></p>
