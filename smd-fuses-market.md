<p><p>SMD (Surface Mount Device) fuses are essential for overcurrent protection in various electronic applications, categorized into AC and DC markets. AC fuses are used predominantly in alternating current circuits, commonly found in household appliances and power supplies, ensuring safety by interrupting excessive current flow. Both types are vital for enhancing the reliability and safety of electronic devices.</p></p>
