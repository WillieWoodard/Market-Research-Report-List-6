<p><p>Lumbar artificial discs are devices used to replace damaged or degenerated discs in the spine. There are two main types of lumbar artificial discs in the market: metal and biopolymer discs. Metal discs are typically made of materials like stainless steel or titanium, providing durability and strength. Biopolymer discs, on the other hand, are made from bioresorbable materials that can be gradually absorbed by the body. Both types of discs aim to restore flexibility and motion to the spine while reducing pain and improving overall quality of life for patients.</p></p>
