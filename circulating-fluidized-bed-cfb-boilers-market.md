<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) boilers are categorized based on their operating pressure and temperature capabilities. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below critical pressure, ensuring efficiency for lower capacities. Supercritical CFB boilers, operating above critical pressure, allow for higher efficiencies and reduced emissions, making them suitable for large-scale power generation. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers go further, achieving even higher temperatures and pressures for maximum efficiency and environmental performance, thereby representing the cutting-edge technology in CFB boiler design for sustainable energy production.</p></p>
