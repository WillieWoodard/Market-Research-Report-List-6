<p><strong>The Non-vascular Lumen Guidewire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Guidewire</li><li>Elbow Guidewire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Straight guidewires are long, flexible wires used in various medical procedures to help guide catheters and other medical instruments through the body's vasculature. Elbow guidewires, on the other hand, have a curved or angled tip that allows for easier navigation through tortuous or hard-to-reach blood vessels. Both types of guidewires are crucial tools in minimally invasive procedures such as angioplasty, stenting, and embolization, providing physicians with better access and precision during treatments.</p></p>
