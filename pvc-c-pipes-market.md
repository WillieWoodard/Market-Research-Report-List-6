<p><p>The PVC-C pipes market is primarily segmented into Schedule 40 and Schedule 80 types, each catering to different application needs. Schedule 40 pipes are typically used for residential and low-pressure applications due to their lighter wall thickness and lower pressure ratings. In contrast, Schedule 80 pipes feature thicker walls, making them suitable for high-pressure environments, industrial applications, and chemical processing. Both types offer excellent corrosion resistance, durability, and ease of installation, contributing to their growing adoption in various sectors.</p></p>
