<p><p>Benchtop pH meters are available in single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel types. Single channel pH meters are designed to measure the pH of one sample at a time, making them suitable for smaller-scale applications. Dual channel pH meters allow for the simultaneous measurement of two samples, increasing efficiency in laboratories with higher testing volumes. Multi-channel pH meters can measure multiple samples at once, making them ideal for busy laboratories that require high throughput and accuracy in pH measurements.</p></p>
