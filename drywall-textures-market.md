<p><p>Drywall textures refer to different finishes applied to drywall surfaces for aesthetic purposes. Some common types include Knockdown, where the texture is created by using a trowel to flatten the texture; Orange Peel, which results in a bumpy texture resembling the skin of an orange; Popcorn, a bumpy texture similar to popcorn kernels; Sand Swirl, where a sand-like texture is swirled onto the surface. Other types of drywall textures include stomp, slap brush, and skip trowel, each creating a unique finish on the drywall surface.</p></p>
