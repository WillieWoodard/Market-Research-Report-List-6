<p><p>A synchronous pulley, also known as a timing pulley, is a mechanical device that is used to transmit motion between two parallel shafts in a synchronous manner. <p><p>Synchronous pulleys come in three main types in the market: Double groove, Single groove, and Multiple groove. Double groove pulleys have two grooves which allow for increased stability and power transmission. Single groove pulleys have one groove and are commonly used for simple applications requiring low power transmission. Multiple groove pulleys have more than two grooves and are suitable for applications where multiple belts need to be used for increased power transmission. Each type of pulley offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
