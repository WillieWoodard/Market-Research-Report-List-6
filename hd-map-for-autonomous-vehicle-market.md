<p><p>The HD Map for Autonomous Vehicle market is segmented into two primary types: cloud-based and embedded. Cloud-based HD maps utilize remote servers to store and update high-definition mapping data, allowing seamless updates and real-time information access for connected vehicles. In contrast, embedded HD maps involve on-board storage within the vehicle, providing immediate access to mapping data without relying on internet connectivity. Each type serves distinct needs; cloud-based offers flexibility and scale, while embedded provides reliability and low latency for critical navigation tasks.</p></p>
