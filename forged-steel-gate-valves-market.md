<p><p>Forged Steel Gate Valves come in three main types based on their bonnet design: Bolted Bonnet, Welded Bonnet, and Pressure Seal Bonnet. Bolted Bonnet valves have a separate body and bonnet held together by bolts, making maintenance easier. Welded Bonnet valves have the body and bonnet welded together, providing a more compact design. Pressure Seal Bonnet valves are used in high-pressure applications and feature a bonnet that self-seals under pressure to prevent leaks. Each type offers specific advantages based on the valve's application.</p></p>
