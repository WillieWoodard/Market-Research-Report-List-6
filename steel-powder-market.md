<p><p>The steel powder market is segmented into three main types: atomization, reduction, and others. Atomization involves transforming molten steel into fine particles through high-pressure gas or water jets, producing high-quality powders suitable for various applications. Reduction refers to the chemical process of converting iron ore into metallic iron powder, typically used in sintering and other metalworking processes. Other methods may include milling and electrolytic processes, catering to specific industry needs and advanced fabrication technologies.</p></p>
