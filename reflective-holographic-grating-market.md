<p><p>Reflective holographic grating market consists of two main types - Flat Type and Concave Type. Flat Type holographic gratings offer a simple and cost-effective solution for various applications like spectroscopy and telecommunications. On the other hand, Concave Type holographic gratings have a curved surface that enables better focusing of the diffracted light, making them ideal for applications requiring high resolution and precision, such as laser systems and astronomical instruments. Both types cater to different needs in the market depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
