<p><ul><li>M-mode Display</li><li>B-mode Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transcranial Doppler Devices Market includes two main types: M-mode Display and B-mode Display. M-mode Display provides a one-dimensional view of blood flow velocities, making it ideal for measuring velocity changes over time. On the other hand, B-mode Display offers a two-dimensional image of blood flow within the brain, allowing for a more detailed visualization of cerebral blood vessels. Both types of displays play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring conditions such as stroke, intracranial stenosis, and vasospasm.</p></p>
