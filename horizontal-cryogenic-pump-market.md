<p><p>The Horizontal Cryogenic Pump market is segmented into Submersible and Non-Submersible types. Submersible pumps are designed to operate while submerged in the cryogenic liquid, offering efficient operation and reduced space requirements. Conversely, Non-Submersible pumps operate outside the cryogenic fluid, often using longer transfer lines, making them suitable for applications where space is not a constraint. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as energy, aerospace, and medical, facilitating the transfer and storage of cryogenic fluids.</p></p>
