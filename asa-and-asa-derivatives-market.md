<p><p>The ASA (Average Settlement Amount) derivatives market comprises various types that cater to different trading needs. OSA (Overnight Settlement Amount) focuses on short-term transactions, while ODSA (On-Demand Settlement Amount) allows for real-time settlements. NSA (Net Settlement Amount) aggregates multiple transactions for netting purposes, enhancing efficiency. DDSA (Daily Settlement Amount) addresses daily adjustments based on market fluctuations. Together, these markets provide flexibility and efficiency in managing settlement processes and risk exposure in derivatives trading.</p></p>
