<p><p>The titanium scrap market comprises several types, each with distinct characteristics. Titanium swarf consists of fine particles resulting from cutting processes. Rutile scraps are derived from titanium dioxide, a key mineral. Titanium sponge refers to a porous titanium form produced through chemical reduction, and residues consist of leftover titanium materials.
