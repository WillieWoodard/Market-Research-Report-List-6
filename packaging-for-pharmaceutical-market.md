<p><p>Packaging in the pharmaceutical market encompasses various materials, each serving distinct purposes. Paper packaging, typically used for inserts and cartons, offers eco-friendliness and cost-effectiveness. Glass, favored for its inert properties, is essential for preserving sensitive medications. Other materials may include biodegradable options or multilayer composites, enhancing sustainability and safety.</p></p>
