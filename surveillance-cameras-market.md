<p><p>Surveillance cameras are categorized into four main types based on functionality and technology. Ordinary type cameras offer basic monitoring with standard video quality. Moon type cameras, designed for low-light environments, provide enhanced visibility at night, akin to moonlight. Starlight type cameras excel in extremely low-light conditions, capturing clear images without supplemental lighting. Infrared lighting type cameras utilize infrared technology to detect heat, enabling clear night vision in total darkness, making them ideal for security in challenging light conditions.</p></p>
