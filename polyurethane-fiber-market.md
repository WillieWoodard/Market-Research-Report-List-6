<p><p>The polyurethane fiber market encompasses various production methods, primarily including solution dry spinning and solution wet spinning. Solution dry spinning involves dissolving polyurethane in a solvent and then extruding it into heated air to form fibers, resulting in lightweight and durable material. In contrast, solution wet spinning involves extruding the polymer solution into a coagulation bath, creating fibers with different properties, often softer and more elastic. Other methods may include melt spinning or electrospinning, contributing to diverse product applications in textiles and industrial uses.</p></p>
