<p><p>Autoimmunity reagents are used for diagnosing autoimmune diseases. The market types include BLOT (Western Blotting), ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), IFA (Immunofluorescence Assay), and CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay). BLOT is used to detect specific proteins in a sample, ELISA measures the concentration of antibodies or antigens, IFA detects antibodies in patient samples, and CLIA utilizes chemiluminescence to detect biomarkers. Each type of reagent offers specific advantages and is utilized in various clinical settings for autoimmune disease diagnosis.</p></p>
