<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Smoke Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke detector market comprises three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms use a light sensor to detect smoke from smoldering fires, making them effective for slow-burning materials. Ionization alarms detect fast-flaming fires using radioactive particles that sense changes in air ionization. Combination smoke alarms integrate both technologies, offering comprehensive protection against different fire types. This variety allows consumers to choose detectors tailored to their specific safety needs and environments.</p></p>
