<p><strong>The Hall Effect Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop Hall Effect Current Sensors</li><li>Closed-Loop Hall Effect Current Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall Effect current sensors are crucial for measuring current in various applications. Open-loop Hall Effect current sensors utilize a magnetic field generated by the current to produce a proportional output voltage, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but with lower accuracy and linearity. In contrast, closed-loop Hall Effect current sensors use a feedback mechanism to enhance precision and linearity, making them suitable for high-performance applications, despite being more complex and expensive. Both types have distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
