<p><p>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) market is categorized into two main types: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal wave sensing primarily detects vibrations and movements along the length of a fiber optic cable, making it ideal for monitoring infrastructure and seismic activities. In contrast, transverse wave sensing captures lateral movements and offers enhanced sensitivity for applications like pipeline monitoring. Both methods leverage fiber optic technology to provide cost-effective and real-time acoustic monitoring solutions across various industries.</p></p>
