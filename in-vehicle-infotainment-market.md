<p><p>The In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI) market comprises three primary types: embedded, tethered, and integrated systems. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle, offering seamless functionality and controls for navigation, entertainment, and connectivity. Tethered systems connect mobile devices to the vehicle, allowing users to access apps and features from their smartphones while driving. Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered functionalities, providing a holistic user experience by blending in-car technology with mobile device capabilities for enhanced entertainment and information access.</p></p>
