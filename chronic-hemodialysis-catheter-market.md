<p><p>The chronic hemodialysis catheter market is categorized by different types of tips and materials. Tip types include step-tip, split-tip, and symmetric tip catheters, each designed for specific vascular access needs. Step-tip catheters allow easier insertion, while split-tip and symmetric tip designs enhance blood flow and reduce thrombosis risk. Materials used vary between silicone and polyurethane (PUR), with silicone offering flexibility and biocompatibility, whereas PUR provides durability and strength, impacting performance and patient comfort during dialysis treatments.</p></p>
