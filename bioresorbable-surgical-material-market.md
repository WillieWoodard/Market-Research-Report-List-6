<p><p>The bioresorbable surgical material market comprises several key types. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is derived from renewable resources and is widely used for sutures and implants due to its biodegradability. Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) offers rapid absorption and strength, making it ideal for temporary implants. Polysaccharides, like chitosan, provide biocompatibility and promote tissue regeneration. Polycaprolactone (PCL) has a slower degradation rate and is used for long-term applications. The "Others" category includes various emerging materials that cater to specific medical needs.</p></p>
