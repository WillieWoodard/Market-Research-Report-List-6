<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) System market is primarily classified into two types: Ultrasound-Guided and MR-Guided. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to target tissues with precision, often used in treating conditions like tumors and fibroids. In contrast, MR-Guided HIFU employs magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced visualization, allowing for precise targeting and treatment monitoring, often preferred in complex cases. Both types focus on delivering non-invasive, targeted therapies, ensuring minimal damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
