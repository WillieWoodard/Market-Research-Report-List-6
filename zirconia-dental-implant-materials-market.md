<p><p>The zirconia dental implant materials market is primarily divided into two types: zirconia dental discs and zirconia dental blocks. Zirconia dental discs are flat, circular pieces used for creating crowns and bridges, offering excellent aesthetics and biocompatibility. In contrast, zirconia dental blocks are larger, cubic forms used for milling dental restorations and implants, providing versatility in design and enhanced strength. Both types are favored for their durability, resistance to corrosion, and ability to mimic the natural appearance of teeth.</p></p>
