<p><p>The drilling and completion fluids market encompasses three main types: water-based systems, oil-based systems, and synthetic-based systems. Water-based systems are eco-friendly and widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of disposal. Oil-based systems offer superior lubricity and stability in high-temperature environments, making them suitable for challenging formations. Synthetic-based systems combine the benefits of both water and oil-based fluids, providing excellent performance while minimizing environmental impact. Each type serves specific drilling needs and enhances operational efficiency.</p></p>
