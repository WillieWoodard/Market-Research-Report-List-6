<p><p>The brake wear indicator market comprises two primary types: electrical indicators and audible indicators. Electrical indicators utilize sensors to monitor brake pad thickness and provide visual alerts to drivers when replacement is needed, enhancing safety. In contrast, audible indicators emit sounds when brake wear reaches a critical level, effectively warning the driver through auditory signals. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle maintenance, ensuring timely brake servicing and preventing potential safety hazards on the road.</p></p>
