<p><ul><li>Circumscribed Type</li><li>Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The virtual reality headset market can be categorized into two main types: circumscribed type and integrated type. Circumscribed type headsets are standalone devices that require external sensors and setups, offering users a more immersive experience with enhanced tracking capabilities, often seen in systems like VR arcade setups. In contrast, integrated type headsets are self-contained, combining everything needed for VR within the headset itself, providing convenience and ease of use, appealing to casual users and those seeking mobile VR experiences.</p></p>
