<p><p>The Spinal Fusion Biologics market encompasses several types of biological materials used to promote spinal fusion procedures. Bone grafts are harvested from the patient or donors and provide structural support while encouraging bone healing. Bone graft substitutes are synthetic or processed materials that mimic the properties of natural bone. Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) involves using a concentration of platelets from the patient's blood to enhance healing. Other market types include various biomaterials and therapies designed to facilitate spinal fusion.</p></p>
