<p><strong>The Digital Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital mass flow controller market is primarily segmented into thermal pressure type and differential pressure type controllers. Thermal pressure types utilize temperature variations to measure mass flow, offering high sensitivity and quick response times, ideal for gases. In contrast, differential pressure types measure the pressure drop across a flow element, providing accurate flow measurements in various applications, including liquids. Both types enhance process control and efficiency across industries like semiconductor, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing, reflecting their growing adoption in modern applications.</p></p>
