<p><p>The Low VCE transistor market is primarily categorized into NPN and PNP types, which differ in their construction and operation. NPN transistors, which have a positive voltage applied to the collector, are more commonly used due to their higher electron mobility, enabling faster switching and better performance in amplification applications. PNP transistors, on the other hand, are activated by a negative voltage at the base, making them suitable for specific applications like low-side switching. Both types serve crucial roles in various electronic circuits.</p></p>
