<p><strong>The Balls Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ball Screw</li><li>External Circulation Ball Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screws are mechanical devices used to convert rotational motion into linear motion. The internal circulation ball screw market involves a design where the balls circulate within the nut rather than the screw shaft, leading to smoother and more precise movement. On the other hand, the external circulation ball screw market features the balls circulating around the screw shaft. Both types offer advantages in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and load capacity, catering to various industrial and automation applications.</p></p>
