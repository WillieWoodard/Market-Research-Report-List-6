<p><p>Loose fill polystyrene packaging encompasses various types tailored for efficient cushioning and protection of products. Ordinary polystyrene is a basic, rigid variant used for general applications. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and offers superior insulation, making it popular for fragile items. High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) combines strength and impact resistance, ideal for demanding packaging needs. Styrene-Polyurethane Styrene (SPS) features elasticity and resilience, enhancing protective qualities against shocks. Together, these materials cater to diverse packaging requirements in various industries.</p></p>
