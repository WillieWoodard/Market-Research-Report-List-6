<p><ul><li>Open Heeled Fins</li><li>Closed Heeled(Full Foot) Fins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scuba fins come in two main types, open heeled fins and closed heeled (full foot) fins. Open heeled fins have an adjustable strap at the back, allowing for a secure fit with diving boots. They are versatile and popular for cold water diving. On the other hand, closed heeled fins are designed like a shoe and do not require additional footwear. They are more compact, lightweight, and often favored by warm water divers for their simplicity and ease of use.</p></p>
