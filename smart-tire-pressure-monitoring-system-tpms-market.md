<p><p>The Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) enhances vehicle safety by continuously monitoring and reporting tire pressure. Market types include Indirect TPMS, which estimates pressure using vehicle dynamics and wheel speed sensors, and Direct TPMS, which utilizes pressure sensors embedded in the tire to provide real-time data. Indirect systems are generally more cost-effective and less complex, while direct systems offer precise, real-time readings, contributing to better fuel efficiency and driving safety, thus influencing consumer preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
