<p><ul><li>Gridded Ion Engine (GIE)</li><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric propulsion systems are categorized into various types based on their design and operation. Gridded Ion Engine (GIE) utilizes a grid to accelerate ions and produce thrust. Hall Effect Thruster (HET) uses magnetic fields to accelerate ions. High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT) is a multi-stage system that produces high thrust with efficiency. Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT) generates thrust through intermittent plasma pulses. Apart from these, there are other types of electric propulsion systems in the market, each offering unique features and advantages for different applications.</p></p>
