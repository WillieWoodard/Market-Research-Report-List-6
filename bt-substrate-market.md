<p><strong>The BT Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>FC CSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BT substrate markets include WB BGA, WB CSP, and FC CSP, each catering to specific packaging needs in the semiconductor industry. WB BGA (Wafer-Level Ball Grid Array) uses water-based processes to improve thermal and electrical performance. WB CSP (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package) offers compact designs for smaller form factors. FC CSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package) allows for high-density interconnects through direct chip attachment, enhancing signal integrity and reducing space, making them essential for advanced electronics applications.</p></p>
