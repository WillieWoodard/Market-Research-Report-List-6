Manual hoists rely on human effort for operation, making them cost-effective but labor-intensive. Electric hoists utilize electricity for efficient and rapid lifting, ideal for heavy loads. Air hoists operate using compressed air, offering lightweight and safe lifting solutions in hazardous environments. Hydraulic hoists leverage fluid power for heavy-duty lifting, providing high strength and control, suitable for industrial applications where precision is essential.</p></p>
