<p><strong>The Handheld Doppler Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Doppler</li><li>Non Directional Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld Doppler ultrasound systems are categorized into directional and non-directional Doppler types. Directional Doppler systems utilize specific angles to measure blood flow velocities and provide detailed information about the direction and speed of blood flow in vessels. In contrast, non-directional Doppler systems detect movement without discerning flow direction, offering a simpler overview of blood flow. Both types are crucial in various medical applications, including vascular assessments and cardiac diagnostics, enhancing patient care through portable and accessible technology.</p></p>
