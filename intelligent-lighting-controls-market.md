<p><p>Intelligent Lighting Controls market includes wired and wireless technologies. Wired technology utilizes physical connections, such as Ethernet cables, to communicate and control lighting systems. This type of technology is known for its reliability and stability. On the other hand, wireless technology enables communication between lighting fixtures through radio frequency or infrared signals, eliminating the need for physical connections. Wireless technology offers flexibility and ease of installation, making it suitable for retrofitting existing buildings or areas where wiring is impractical.</p></p>
