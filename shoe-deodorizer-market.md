<p><ul><li>Powder Shoe Deodorizer</li><li>Spray Shoe Deodorizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoe deodorizer market primarily consists of two types: powder and spray. Powder shoe deodorizers are granular substances that absorb moisture and odors effectively; they are often sprinkled inside shoes and left to work overnight. Spray shoe deodorizers, on the other hand, are liquid solutions packaged in aerosol or pump bottles, providing a quick and convenient way to eliminate odors by spraying directly inside the footwear. Both types target odor control but offer different application methods suited to consumer preferences.</p></p>
