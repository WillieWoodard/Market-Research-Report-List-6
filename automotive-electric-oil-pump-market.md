<p><p>The automotive electric oil pump market is segmented into two main types: separate pumps and integrated pumps. Separate pumps function independently to deliver oil to the engine, offering more flexibility in design and installation. They are often used in high-performance applications where precise oil delivery is crucial. Integrated pumps, on the other hand, are built into other engine components, saving space and reducing weight. This integration can enhance overall efficiency and provide better performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
