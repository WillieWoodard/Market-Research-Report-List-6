<p><p>The biochemical sensors market encompasses four main types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemical sensors**, which detect chemical reactions through electrical signals, are widely used in medical diagnostics and environmental monitoring.</p><p>2. **Thermal sensors** measure temperature changes associated with biochemical reactions, important for diagnosing metabolic conditions.</p><p>3. **Piezoelectric sensors** utilize mechanical stress to identify mass changes on sensor surfaces, ideal for detecting biomolecules.</p><p>4. **Optical sensors** exploit light interactions with biological samples for non-invasive analysis, commonly used in healthcare and food safety applications.</p></p>
