<p><strong>The Polyester Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester strapping is available in two main types: embossed PET strapping and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping features textured surfaces that enhance grip and tensioning, making it suitable for demanding applications and heavy loads. In contrast, smooth PET strapping offers ease of handling and friction resistance, ideal for lighter packaging tasks. Both types are durable, resistant to moisture and UV rays, and widely used in industries such as logistics, manufacturing, and construction for secure bundling and palletizing.</p></p>
