<p><strong>The Pump in Lubrication Application Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Rotary Vane Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In lubrication applications, various pump types are utilized to ensure effective oil circulation. Internal gear pumps feature two gears that create a sealed chamber, ideal for high-viscosity fluids. External gear pumps use two intermeshing gears to move lubricants efficiently. Rotary vane pumps employ sliding vanes within a rotor to maintain consistent flow, suitable for low-viscosity fluids. Screw pumps utilize rotating screws to create a continuous flow, excelling in high-pressure applications. Each type is chosen based on specific viscosity and application requirements.</p></p>
