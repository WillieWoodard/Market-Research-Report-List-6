<p><p>Programmable air sampling pumps are divided into two main types based on flow rate: high flow pumps and low flow pumps. High flow pumps are designed to collect larger volumes of air at a faster rate, making them suitable for sampling in environments with high levels of pollutants. On the other hand, low flow pumps are used for sampling in situations where precise measurements of contaminants at low concentrations are required. Both types of pumps offer programmable features for customized sampling protocols.</p></p>
