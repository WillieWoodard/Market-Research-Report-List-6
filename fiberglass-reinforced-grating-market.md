<p><strong>The Fiberglass Reinforced Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Molding Type</li><li>Extrusion Molding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Grating market offers two main types - Compression Molding Type and Extrusion Molding Type. Compression Molding Type involves the process of molding fiberglass and resin under high pressure to create grating with high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, Extrusion Molding Type involves pulling heated mixture of fiberglass and resin through a die to form the grating shape. Both types cater to different needs and applications in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and marine.</p></p>
