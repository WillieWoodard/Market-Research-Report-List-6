<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power MOSFET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode Power MOSFET</li><li>Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power MOSFET market is divided into two primary types: Depletion Mode and Enhancement Mode Power MOSFETs. Depletion Mode MOSFETs are normally on devices that can conduct current without a gate voltage, making them suitable for low-power applications where power loss needs to be minimized. In contrast, Enhancement Mode MOSFETs are normally off and require a positive gate voltage to conduct, offering higher efficiency and control in high-power applications. Both types are crucial for various electronic devices.</p></p>
