<p><p>The potentiometers market is primarily divided into linear and rotary types. Linear potentiometers regulate resistance based on linear movement, often used in applications like sliders and volume controls. Conversely, rotary potentiometers adjust resistance through rotational movement, commonly found in knobs and dials. Both types are essential in various sectors, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, enabling precise control of voltage and signal levels. Their diverse functionalities drive ongoing demand and innovation in the market.</p></p>
