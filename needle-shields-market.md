<p><p>Needle Shields can be classified into two main types: Rigid Needle Shields and Flexible Needle Shields. </p><p>Rigid Needle Shields are made of hard material and provide strong protection for the needle. They are typically used in settings where a high level of needle protection is required, such as hospitals and clinics.</p><p>Flexible Needle Shields, on the other hand, are made of more pliable material and offer a more adaptable fit for different types of needles. They are often used in home healthcare settings where convenience and ease of use are priorities. Both types of Needle Shields play a crucial role in preventing needlestick injuries and maintaining safety in healthcare environments.</p></p>
