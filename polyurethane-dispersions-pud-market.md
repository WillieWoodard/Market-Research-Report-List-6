<p><p>Polyurethane Dispersions (PUD) are a type of polyurethane resin that is dispersed in water. There are three main types of PUD markets: One-Component Polyurethane, Two-Component Polyurethane, and Urethane-modified. One-Component Polyurethane is a ready-to-use system that cures with moisture in the air. Two-Component Polyurethane consists of a resin and a hardener that need to be mixed before application. Urethane-modified PUDs contain both urethane and non-urethane components, offering enhanced performance properties such as improved abrasion resistance and flexibility.</p></p>
