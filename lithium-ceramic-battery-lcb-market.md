The market comprises primarily two types: laminate and cylindrical. Laminate-type LCBs feature flat, layered construction, ideal for applications requiring compact dimensions and flexibility, such as in electric vehicles and portable electronics. Cylindrical-type LCBs, with their robust cylindrical design, are preferred for high-capacity applications, offering excellent mechanical stability and efficiency, often utilized in larger energy storage systems and automotive uses.</p></p>
