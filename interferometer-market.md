<p><p>Interferometers are devices used to measure wavelength shifts, refractive index variations, and tiny changes in distance. The main types include Laser Fizeau, Mach-Zehnder, Michelson, Fabry-Perot, Sagnac, and Twyman-Green Laser Interferometers. Each type has unique features and applications, such as high precision in Laser Fizeau, versatile design in Mach-Zehnder, compactness in Michelson, high resolution in Fabry-Perot, rotational sensitivity in Sagnac, and suitability for testing optical components in Twyman-Green. Other interferometer types also exist to cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
