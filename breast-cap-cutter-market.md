<p><p>Breast cap cutters are tools used in the poultry industry to remove breast caps from chicken carcasses. There are two main types of breast cap cutters- manual control and automatic cutting. Manual control breast cap cutters require the operator to manually control the cutting process, while automatic cutting breast cap cutters are programmed to cut the breast caps automatically without the need for manual intervention. Both types of breast cap cutters offer efficient and precise breast cap removal, but automatic cutting breast cap cutters are more suitable for high-volume processing.</p></p>
