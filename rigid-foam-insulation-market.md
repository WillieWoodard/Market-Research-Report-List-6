<p><ul><li>Expanded Polystyrene (EPS)</li><li>Extruded Polystyrene (XPS)</li><li>Polyisocyanurate</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid foam insulation market comprises several key types, each suited for specific applications. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and cost-effective, offering good insulation at lower thicknesses. Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) provides higher moisture resistance and compressive strength, making it ideal for below-grade applications. Polyisocyanurate offers high thermal resistance and is commonly used in roofing systems. Other types include polyurethane and phenolic foam, which provide specialized insulation properties. Each type addresses unique needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
