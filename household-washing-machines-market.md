<p><p>Household washing machines come in two main types - fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic washing machines require little to no manual intervention as they automatically wash, rinse, and spin the clothes with the press of a button. They are convenient and user-friendly but tend to be more expensive. On the other hand, semi-automatic washing machines require manual effort to move clothes from washing to spinning tubs. They are more affordable but require more involvement in the washing process. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
