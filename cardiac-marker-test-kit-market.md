<p><p>Cardiac Marker Test Kit market can be segregated into three types based on the sample used for testing - Whole Blood, Serum, and Plasma. Whole Blood testing involves analyzing a blood sample that has not been separated into its components, making it a convenient choice for point-of-care testing. Serum testing involves using a blood sample where the blood has clotted and the liquid part without cells is analyzed. Plasma testing involves analyzing the liquid portion of blood with anticoagulants added to prevent clotting. Each type has its own advantages based on the specific testing requirements.</p></p>
