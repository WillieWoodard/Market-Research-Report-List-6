<p><p>ECG Leadwires come in various types such as Angled, J Shape, and Straight leads. Angled leads have a bend in them to allow for easier connection and movement. J Shape leads have a hook-like shape for secure attachment to the electrodes. Straight leads are simply straight wire connections for the electrodes. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of flexibility, ease of use, and stability during monitoring. Health care professionals can choose the type of leadwire based on the specific needs of the patient and the monitoring equipment.</p></p>
