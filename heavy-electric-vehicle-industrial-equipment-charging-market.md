<p><p>The Heavy Electric Vehicle and Industrial Equipment Charging Market comprises two main types of power transfer: static and dynamic. Static Power Transfer involves fixed charging infrastructure, where vehicles connect to charging stations while stationary, ideal for fleet management and depot applications. Dynamic Power Transfer, on the other hand, enables charging while the vehicle is in motion, utilizing technologies like overhead wires or in-road inductive systems. This facilitates continuous operation without downtime, boosting efficiency for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
