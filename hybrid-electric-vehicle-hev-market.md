<p><strong>The Hybrid Electric Vehicle (HEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Electric Vehicle</li><li>Series Hybrid Electric Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine conventional internal combustion engines with electric propulsion to enhance efficiency and reduce emissions. In the Parallel Hybrid configuration, both the electric motor and engine can drive the wheels, optimizing power and fuel economy. The Series Hybrid, on the other hand, uses the internal combustion engine solely to generate electricity for the electric motor, making it more efficient in urban settings. Both types cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in the growing eco-friendly vehicle market.</p></p>
