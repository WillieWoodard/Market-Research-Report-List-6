<p><p>The Machine Vision Smart Cameras market is primarily categorized into Smart CCD Cameras and Smart CMOS Cameras. Smart CCD Cameras utilize charge-coupled device technology, offering high-resolution imaging and excellent light sensitivity, making them ideal for precision applications. In contrast, Smart CMOS Cameras leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and greater integration capabilities. Both types play critical roles in industrial automation, quality control, and robotics, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of machine vision systems.</p></p>
