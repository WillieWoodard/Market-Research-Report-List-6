<p><p>DNA electrophoresis systems are essential laboratory tools used for separating DNA fragments based on size. Horizontal gel electrophoresis systems utilize agarose gels to efficiently separate larger DNA fragments, making them ideal for applications like PCR and genomic studies. In contrast, vertical gel electrophoresis systems employ polyacrylamide gels, allowing for high-resolution separation of smaller DNA fragments and proteins. Both systems cater to various research needs, enhancing the capabilities of genetic analysis and molecular biology applications.</p></p>
